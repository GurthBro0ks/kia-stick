# KIA-Stick-v0.9.9-stop-on-warn-fail-closeout-guard

Product version: `0.7.0`
Prompt version: `prompt.fake-docs.v0.5-import-wizard-hardening`
Queue item: `queue-054-v099-stop-on-warn-fail-closeout-guard`

## Purpose

This phase makes bundle and closeout guidance harder to misuse when `WARN` or `FAIL` appears.

## Stop Rule

- If proof, report, validation, queue, or safety output says `WARN`, stop and review before continuing.
- If proof, report, validation, queue, or safety output says `FAIL`, stop and fix before continuing.
- A queue item must not move to accepted while unresolved `WARN` or `FAIL` evidence exists.
- Missing proof may be `WARN` unless replacement proof is explicitly recorded and validation passed.
- Replacement proof must stay GitHub-safe and must not copy or inspect private files.

## Helper Behavior

`scripts/closeout-helper.mjs` remains read-only. It reviews proof, git, and queue state; it does not mutate queue state and does not push.

The helper now prints:

- `stop_on_warn_fail=true` when closeout is not clear.
- `queue_acceptance_allowed=false` when closeout is not clear.
- `queue_acceptance_allowed=true` only when proof, git, and queue state are clear.

No runtime behavior is changed.
