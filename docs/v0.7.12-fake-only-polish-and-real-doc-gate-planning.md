## Planning-Only Real-Doc Gate Prep

This section prepares future review language only. It does not approve implementation.

Future real-doc work must use an exactly-one-document and exactly-one-gate concept:

- Exactly one future gate is named in the future prompt.
- Exactly one future document is named by the operator in a separate approval packet.
- The approved action is narrow and reversible.
- All blocked actions are listed before work begins.
- The proof plan is GitHub-safe before work begins.
- The stop conditions are explicit before work begins.

Required future operator approval packet fields:

- Operator name or local signoff handle.
- Approval date and time.
- Exact phase name.
- Exact one-document scope.
- Exact one-gate action.
- Explicit blocked actions.
- Safety checklist result.
- Redaction/privacy policy result.
- Rollback and deletion/retention plan.
- Proof-safe output agreement.
- Stop-on-WARN/FAIL agreement.

Required future safety checklist requirements:

- Confirm rollback and deletion/retention steps are written before the gate starts.
